Career

Rohit Sharma is regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of the modern era. Born on 30 April 1987 in Mumbai, he started playing cricket at a very young age and joined the Mumbai Cricket Academy at just 11 years old.

Early Domestic Career

Rohit began his professional journey through age-group cricket. He represented Delhi in U-15, U-17, and U-19 tournaments, consistently delivering remarkable performances. His major breakthrough came when he captained the India U-19 team to World Cup victory in 2006 in Malaysia. His aggression, batting style, and leadership brought him into the spotlight.

International Debut (2007)

Rohit made his ODI debut for India on 18 August 2008 against Sri Lanka. Initially playing as a reserve opener, he soon proved his consistency and hunger for runs. By 2010, he became a regular part of the Indian team.

Rise to Stardom

From 2011 onwards, Rohit became India's most reliable batsman. He played a crucial role in the 2011 ICC Cricket World Cup victory, scoring a half-century in the final.

Between 2013-2019, Rohit dominated world cricket and was ranked as the No.1 batsman across formats multiple times. His aggressive playing style, unmatched fitness, and chase-master ability earned him the title **"Hitman"**.

Test Career & Captaincy

Rohit made his Test debut in 2011 and became the Test captain in 2014 after MS Dhoni's retirement from the format.

As captain, he transformed India into a world-class Test team. Under his leadership:

  • India became the No.1 Test team for **5 consecutive years (2016–2021)**.
  • India won a historic Test series in Australia in **2018–19**.
  • He introduced high fitness standards through the Yo-Yo Test.
IPL Career

Rohit sharma has been part of the Royal Challengers Bangalore (RCB) since the first IPL season in 2008. His best season was in 2016 when he scored **973 runs**, the highest in IPL history, including 4 centuries in one season.

Milestones & Records
  • Only player to average 50+ in all formats simultaneously.
  • Most runs in a single IPL season (973 in 2016).
  • Most centuries in run chases.
  • First Indian to score 3 consecutive ODI centuries twice.
  • Over 25,000 runs in international cricket.
Current Role

Rohit sharma continues to play as a senior batsman for India. He remains one of the fittest and most influential modern cricketers and a global sports icon.

Marriage Life

Rohit sharma's marriage to Sports Manager Ritika is one of the most celebrated star unions in India. Their relationship gained attention because both are extremely successful in their respective fields—Virat in cricket and Anushka in acting and film production.

How They Met

Rohit and Ritika first met in 2013 during the shooting of a TV commercial for a shampoo brand. Their natural chemistry during the shoot sparked the beginning of their friendship, which soon turned into a relationship. Though they kept their relationship private initially, their appearances together made headlines.

Relationship Timeline
  • 2013: First meet during advertisement shoot.
  • 2014-2016: Started appearing publicly together.
  • 2017: Rohit openly supported Ritika against media criticism.
  • 11 December 2017: Couple got married in Tuscany, Italy.
The Royal Wedding

The wedding took place at the stunning Borgo Finocchieto in Tuscany, Italy. It was a private, intimate event attended by close family and friends. The couple wore traditional Sabyasachi outfits, and their wedding photos went viral globally within minutes.

After returning to India, they hosted two grand receptions:

  • Delhi Reception: Attended by the Prime Minister of India.
  • Mumbai Reception: Attended by cricketers, Bollywood stars, and industry icons.
Family Life

Virat and Anushka welcomed their first child, a daughter named Vamika, on 11 January 2021. The couple has chosen to keep her away from public attention and paparazzi, requesting privacy for her growing years.

In 2024, the couple welcomed their second child—a baby boy named JR Rohit. Both parents often express how becoming parents has brought new meaning to their lives.

Support & Understanding

Ritika has supported Rohit through his cricket journey, including his highs and lows, and Virat has always praised her for being his strength and biggest supporter. Their relationship is often admired for its balance, mutual respect, and emotional support.

Public Image

Today, Rohit and Ritika are considered one of India’s strongest and most graceful celebrity couples. They appear together in brand endorsements, social campaigns, and important events, inspiring millions of fans with their positivity, humility, and teamwork both in marriage and life.
